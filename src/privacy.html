Your data is used only for professional relationship communication: replying to your inquiry, discussing possible collaboration, defining scope, and following up on consultancy-related topics.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

Processing is based on pre-contractual steps requested by you (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR), legitimate interest in business communication (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), and legal obligations where applicable (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR).

Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Data may be processed by essential service providers used for communication and hosting (for example, email infrastructure) only when necessary for service operation and legal compliance.

This website is informational and does not use personal-data marketing forms. If analytics or cookies are introduced in the future, this page will be updated accordingly.

Retention

Correspondence is kept only for as long as needed to manage ongoing or potential professional relationships, and for legal/accounting obligations when required.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability (where applicable). You may also lodge a complaint with the Portuguese supervisory authority, CNPD.

How to Contact Me
