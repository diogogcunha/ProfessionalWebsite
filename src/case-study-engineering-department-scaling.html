Scaling into a Multi-Service Engineering Department

Market Engineering Organization Transformation
Scale 25 to 100+ Team Members, 5 Services
Business Impact €150K to €750K Profit Margin

The Challenge

Without a structured operating model, the company risked margin erosion, leadership bottlenecks, and inconsistent delivery quality for global clients. An already operating company had a growing engineering team, but no formal service model, limited internal accountability structures, and increasing pressure to deliver consistently across multiple industries.

Over an 18-month transformation, the organization needed to evolve from a single-team setup into a full engineering department capable of scaling sustainably, promoting internal leadership, and maintaining strong financial performance while delivering high-quality outcomes globally.

My Role

I personally led the department redesign, defined service boundaries, and set management operating cadences tied to business KPIs. I coached new engineering managers, ran performance and delivery reviews, and partnered with commercial leadership on margin discipline while teams executed delivery across the five services.

The Solution

Over 18 months, I led a department-wide transformation focused on organization design, leadership development, and service clarity.

1. Service-Based Operating Model

We restructured the organization from an informal team setup into five clearly defined engineering services. Each service had explicit ownership boundaries, accountability mechanisms, and measurable delivery objectives.

2. Leadership and Management Pipeline

We built a development path for engineering managers, with coaching and practical leadership training tied to real delivery responsibilities. This enabled internal promotions while keeping execution quality stable during growth.

3. Quality and Financial Discipline at Scale

We aligned day-to-day engineering operations with business goals, balancing delivery quality, client satisfaction, and margin targets. The department delivered for complex international clients across banking, insurance, fintech, energy, and retail.

The Outcome

  • Team Scale: Expanded the engineering organization from 25 to 100+ professionals.
  • Operating Clarity: Established 5 formal engineering services with clear accountability and internal structure.
  • Financial Growth: Increased profit margin from €150K to €750K while scaling capacity over 18 months.
  • Delivery Quality: Maintained high delivery standards for global clients in regulated and high-demand sectors.
